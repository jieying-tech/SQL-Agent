## Financial Metrics
* **Gross Margin:** Assumed to be 30% of Revenue.
* **Cost of Goods Sold (COGS):** Assumed to be 70% of Revenue.
* **Revenue:** Calculated as sum of `Products.price` * `Order_Items.quantity`.
* **Profit:** Calculated as Revenue - COGS.
* **Average Order Value (AOV):** Average Revenue of orders.

## Customers
* **VIP customer:** A customer whose order totals exceeds $1000.
* **Repeat customer:** A customer who made two or more orders.
* **Churned customer:** A customer whose most recent order is more than 30 days ago.
* **Customer Lifetime Value (CLV):** Total Revenue generated by a specific customer across all their orders.

## Products
* **Active Product:** A product with at least 1 unit sold within the last 30 days.
* **Underperforming Product:** A product with less than 3 units sold in the last 60 days.
